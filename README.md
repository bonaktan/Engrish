# Engrish
Fix your English with Engrish so that it turns to English

## Legalese
ENGRISH is an application designed to utilize AI technologies to (hopefully) teach you English by correcting your mistakes made during application usage.

During this time (October 2024 - May 2025), this application is only being developed for research purposes only. Further developments after that time will be not guaranteed by the researchers.
